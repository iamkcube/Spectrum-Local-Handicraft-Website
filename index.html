Our Story

Our Story

Hastakala comes from a place and time forgotten. Our story is one of revival and reconnection to craft. Get connected to the Hastakala way of life through a journey into the Hastakala story - how we came to be, our memories, dreams, ambitions and ensemble of what our brand stands for.

Our Craft

Our Craft

Our founding premise is to pull-out the forgotten indigenous art from oblivion into your décor. At Hastakala our craft is an elegant blend of heritage and contemporary. Discover more about our craft, the telling conceptualisation, and anecdotal motifs.

Our Team

Our Team

An enthusiastic group of individuals from differing facets joined hands with a common purpose - to revive the heritage art forms and provide an outlet for their masterful artisans.
